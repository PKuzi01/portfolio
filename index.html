Here's a bit about myself:

Yeah. It's Phila, pronounced Pee Lah. You can use this instead of the full one.

In my teens, my mother left me in charge of taking care of the house computer, seeing how I would do well with fixing the other technological appliances like the dvd player. Safe to say, learning about the house computer, and maintaining the old machine exposed me to a lot about computers. All of that over the years opened my curiosity about software development. And here we are.

That it's an artistic medium, if you choose to see that way. More so, that when you create these things, be it apps or websites, you can create for the users, as well for others that create just like you do. It's expansive, yet it has a strong sense of community. There's a subtly wonderful understanding that we're all creators. I enjoy that a lot.

I'm very interested in the UI space. I like the idea of making sketches of the projects, and the idea of protyping and the various ways you can do it seem wonderful! I love styling the websites I build. I find researching for colour palettes and fonts, putting the things together on a css stylesheet, all the fun artsy stuff very entertaining. I'm very much visual in that way, so I wish to one day learn about it and become proficient in that regard.

If I'm not finishing off my undergrad or working on my web developement skills, I am reading, doing yoga, painting, baking, watching anime, spending time with my doggo Jazz, and spending time with all my loved ones.

My favourite colour is green, I love green, I want a green everything. My favourite anime? There's ten. In this order: Darling in the Franxx, Attack on Titan, Hunter x Hunter, Banana Fish, Assassination Classroom, Sk8 t0 Infinity, the Promised Neverland (although this might change, because season 2 is looking iffy), Kakegurui, No Game No Life, and Death Parade. My favourite fruit are (yeah there's multiple for a lot of these) dragonfruit, mango and blood orange. My favourite flowers are tulips, sunflowers and wisteria. My favourite cars are the 2021 Ford Mustang GT500 and the 2020 BMW M4 Competition. My favourite number is 9. My favourite angel numbers are 222 and 888.

I can neither confirm nor deny any answer, thank you for your time. *walks off the stage*
